2. Returns Window

You may return unused products within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Requirements:

  • Items must be unused, in their original condition and packaging with all tags attached.
  • Proof of purchase is required.
  • Items showing signs of use, wear, or alteration cannot be refunded.
  • For health and safety reasons, supplement products can only be returned if the seal is unbroken and the packaging is intact.
  • Final-sale items, where designated, are not eligible for return.

3. Bundle & Promotional Purchases

Items purchased as part of a multi-piece bundle or promotion follow the same 30-day unused-return policy. If only part of a bundle is returned, the remaining items will be repriced at their standard rate, and the difference deducted from the refund.
